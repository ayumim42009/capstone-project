Lemon Ricotta Cookies with Lemon Glaze

Ingredients:
  • 313 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.3 teaspoons baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoons salt
  • 113 grams unsalted butter, softened
  • 150 grams granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest (about 2 lemons)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 180 grams powdered sugar (for glaze)
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice (for glaze)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest (for glaze)
Steps:
  1. 1. Mix dry ingredients: Whisk together 313 grams all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 0.3 teaspoons baking soda, and 0.5 teaspoons salt in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  2. 2. Cream butter and sugar: Beat 113 grams unsalted butter, softened and 150 grams granulated sugar together with an electric mixer on medium-high speed until light and fluffy, about 2–3 minutes.
  3. 3. Add wet ingredients: Add 2 large eggs one at a time, beating after each. Mix in 1 cup whole milk ricotta cheese, 2 tablespoons lemon zest, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract until combined.
  4. 4. Combine and chill: Fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined. Cover the dough and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (up to 24 hours). Do not skip this step — chilling prevents the cookies from spreading.
  5. 5. Preheat oven: Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  6. 6. Scoop and bake: Scoop 1 tablespoon of dough per cookie onto the baking sheets, spacing them about 5cm apart. Bake for 13–15 minutes until the bottoms are lightly golden. The tops will stay pale.
  7. 7. Cool: Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before glazing.
  8. 8. Make glaze and finish: Whisk together 180 grams powdered sugar, 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice, and 1 teaspoon lemon zest until smooth. Dip or spoon glaze over each cooled cookie. Allow glaze to set before storing.
